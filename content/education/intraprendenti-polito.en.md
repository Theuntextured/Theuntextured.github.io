Selected for the [Percorso Intraprendenti](https://www.polito.it/didattica/polito/learning-experiences-in-interdisciplinarity/percorso-intraprendenti) (Honours track), an excellence programme at Politecnico di Torino that I follow alongside my main degree.

This innovative three-year track is reserved for **200 engineering students** and heavily emphasizes working in **multidisciplinary teams**. It aims to boost problem-solving capabilities, soft skills, and sustainability awareness through experimental labs and active collaboration across different engineering disciplines.
